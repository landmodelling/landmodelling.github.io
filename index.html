Advancing Land Modeling for Gulf Coast Resilience

August 14–15, 2025  |  Austin, Texas (PRC Campus, UT Austin) & Virtual

View Participants

Join us for a dynamic workshop hosted by The University of Texas at Austin to advance community‑driven land modeling and foster partnerships addressing environmental challenges in Texas and the Gulf Coast.

Key Details

  • Date: August 14–15, 2025
  • Location: ROC 1.603 (PRC 196), 10601 Exploration Way, Austin, TX 78758, The University of Texas at Austin (see parking and campus maps at the end of this document)
  • Participants: 255 registered (72 in‑person, 183 virtual)

Notes: All times are U.S. Central Daylight Time (CDT). Breakfast, lunch, and dinner provided on both days. International virtual talks are pre‑recorded (12 minutes). Virtual sessions will be recorded for asynchronous viewing. A poster session is scheduled on Day 1, with posters accessible throughout. A panel discussion will be held each day to synthesize key findings for Gulf Coast resilience. Sessions end by 5:30 PM when possible (no later than 6:00 PM). Lunch breaks are ~1 hour.

Venue Location

Pickle Research Campus, UT Austin
10100 Burnet Rd, Austin, TX 78758
