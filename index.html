Advancing Land Modeling for Gulf Coast Resilience

Workshop: August 14–15, 2025 | Austin, Texas

Register Your Interest

Why Attend?

  • Tackle Critical Challenges: Collaborate using Noah-MP for extreme weather modeling.
  • Build Partnerships: Engage academics, agencies, and communities.
  • Drive Impact: Co-create predictive tools for vulnerable communities.
  • Advance Science: Enhance models with participatory, data-driven approaches.

Key Details

  • Date: August 14–15, 2025
  • Location: PRC Campus, UT Austin
  • Participants: ~60 in person + online attendees
  • Activities: Interactive sessions, hands-on modeling, breakout discussions
  • Funded by: UCAR, Jackson School of Geosciences

Expected Outcomes

  • Strengthened collaboration across sectors
  • Inclusive solutions for resilience
  • Open-access tools and a global-use Python package
  • Increased diversity and policy relevance in modeling
